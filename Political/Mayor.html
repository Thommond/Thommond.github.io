My mission

I want to help society forward. I want to see my change in the making. I want to run for mayor. Unlike most jobs you can have an impact on everyone in society directly. I want to run for mayor to spread the belief of liberty, freedom, and the pursue of happiness. If I every have the previlege to be a mayor I hope to give people inspiration about the future and let others decide their path.

Why Mayor?

I know that the responsibility of congress and governer may overwhelm me for my first public service. I want to effect a small group of people so I can focus on their problems to make life easier for them. I can solve the problems the few which is just as important as serving the needs of the many. When you are mayor people thank you face-to-face or tell you their struggles and you can truly connect.

Why me?

Why am I worthy of being a mayor? I can't say I am the best or that I am the one to change the town better than ever before. However, That is my goal. I want to stick up for the individual and support the people at every possible turn. Together is how we change for the better.
