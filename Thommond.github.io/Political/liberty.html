Lets start with polices

The N.A.P

The N.A.P stands for the Non-Aggression Pact. This means it is an agreed upon law which states anyone who shows aggression in the form of violence is violating the law. Not just getting an gry, its more than that. N.A.P. violations include assualt, harra ssment,rape, theft, violation of contract, murder and etc. As you can see its not as simple as it my sound. More on the N.A.P at Wikipedia.

Gun policy

Libertarian gun policy is actual quite simple. We support the right to own, and use your firearm and other weapons if needed. Moreover, Libertarians don't support any weapons ban including weapons attachments and much more. It's your right what autho rity to we have to stop you?

Drug policy

Libertarian drug policy is a bit more complicated. We support the decriminalization of all drugs and the legalization of cannabis and pychoactive mushrooms. We support changing the goal from punishment to rehabilitation with non-criminal drug offenders.

Healthcare Policy

Many Libertarians support the complete abolishment of government healthcare, although I agree in long term measures I would like to prepose a 5 year plan to abolish the federal healthcare system. what would replace it? The free market will allow for cheap alternatives.

Taxes and fees

Libertarian taxation polices are much more leinient then our counterparts. We propose taxes as low as 14% flat rate. Why flat rate? Rich tax payers don't pay 90% of their taxes due to cheap avenues of excape. With a flat rate it would not be worth their time avoiding the tax. I personally support the abolishment of the property, sales, and luxuries taxes.

Foriegn policy

Libertarian foreign policy is straight to the point. Libertatians understand the importance of a strong military, but we are isolation ists. Military should only be for defence and needed conflict. At the current military state Libertarians would highly decrease our footprint in foriegn wars, and establish bases only in defensive positions. The seeking of wars will stop. Moreover, other foreign policy includes the complete defunding of foreign aid in a 3 year period.

Public programs

Libertarians support the end of welfare and other programs that drain the national and local budget. It would be in a periodic fashion for about 5 years. Why? Most program for example SNAP are not achieving their intended goal and instead are having negative effects including, corruption, malpractice, and bottle necking.

Libertarain Core beliefs

Indivdual choice

What Many Libertarians and I support is absolute freedom. we believe that the rights of indivdual people are most important. Libertarians have the thought of the people and their welfare in every decison we make. We believe that individuals are responsible for themselves and nothing more. This does not mean collaberation is not as important but Libertarians acknowledge the choice that every person needs.

Do no harm

Many Libertarians including myself feel obligated to do no harm at consequence. We do not condone any action that leads or causes to the intentional harm of another individual. That is why we created the N.A.P. to keep others and ourselves in check.

Free markets

Libertarians recognize the importance of a centralized power but stress the need of free interaction. A great portion of what Libertarins strive for is people selling, producing, trading and living without government being in every part of their lives. We don't hold grudges or bottle neck our competition out of the spot light. Thats the power of the indivdual.
